How to make a sponge cake

Ever wanted to make a tasty sponge cake but it's too complicated? Read this tutorial for and easy recipie for a sponge cake!

Ingredients:

3 eggs

1g salt

2ml vanilla extract (optional)

90g sugar

90g cake flour/all purpose flour

30g vegetable oil

30g milk

Recipe:

1. Separate egg whites from egg yolks

2. Mix egg yolks with with salt and vanilla extract

3. Beat egg whites until white, goopy stuff

4. Add sugar 1/3 at a time; mix for a while after adding each batch

5. Add egg yolk mixture. Mix well.

6. Add cake flour/all purpose flour (sifted)

7. Gently mix from bottom to top until well blended and no lumps

8. Mix some cake batter with mixture of vegetable oil and milk

9. Combine with remaining batter. Mix well.

10. Put in cake pan (well greased) Destroy bubbles with toothpick/and/or slamming pot.

11. Bake in preheated oven at 170c (338f) for 30 minutes

12. Cool upside down and cut out of pot

Congratulations! You've made your ez sponge cake!

Alternative recipie:

Top recipie still too hard for u? Here's and even ez(er) sponge cake!

Note: This sponge cake quality may not be as good as the one above. Also, NEVER use a hand whisker, only use a electrical one.

Using the hand whisker will result in hours of long work while an electrical one will only use 5 minuites. PROCEED WITH CAUTION!

Ingredients:

4 eggs

80g white sugar

1 tsp of vanilla extract

80g flour or all purpose flour

Recipe:

1. Crack all eggs into a bowl

2. Add all the sugar into eggs

3. Beat until slightly thick, white goopy stuff. ONLY USE ELECTRICAL MIXER!

4. Add vanilla extract

5. Add sifted flour

6. Gently fix/fold until well combined. Do not use force.

7. Pour in cake tin

8. Destroy bubbles with toothpick and slam on table to destroy more

9. Bake at 160c for 25-30 minutes

10. Leave to cool upside down

11. Cut out of tin

EZEZEZEZEZEZEZEZEZEZEZEZEZ SPONGE CAKE!
