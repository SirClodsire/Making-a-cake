How to make a sponge cake

Ever wanted to make a tasty sponge cake but it's too complicated? Read this tutorial for and easy recipie for a sponge cake!

Ingredients:

3 eggs

1g salt

2ml vanilla extract (optional)

90g sugar

90g cake flour/all purpose flour

30g vegetable oil

30g milk

Recipie:

1. Separate egg whites from egg yolks

2. Mix egg yolks with with salt and vanilla extract

3. Beat egg whites until white, goopy stuff

4. Add sugar 1/3 at a time; mix for a while after adding each batch

5. Add egg yolk mixture. Mix well.

6. Add cake flour/all purpose flour (sifted)

7. Gently mix from bottom to top until well blended and no lumps

8. Mix some cake batter with mixture of vegetable oil and milk

9. Combine with remaining batter. Mix well.

10. Put in cake pan (well greased) Destroy bubbles with toothpick/and/or slamming pot.

11. Bake in preheated oven at 170c (338f) for 30 minutes

12. Cool upside down and cut out of pot

Congratulations! You've made your ez sponge cake!
